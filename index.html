Empowering Women Against Violence

Supporting survivors and promoting gender equality in Tunisia

We are a group of dedicated students working to create a safer world for women through awareness campaigns, empowerment programs, and community support.

How We Help

Awareness Campaigns

Educational initiatives to raise awareness about gender-based violence and promote prevention in universities and communities.

Empowerment Programs

Skills development and confidence-building workshops to help women become independent and self-reliant.

Therapy & Support

Professional counseling and emotional support services for survivors of violence in a safe, confidential environment.

About EVAH

EVAH is a student-led organization dedicated to supporting women who face physical violence in Tunisia. We believe in creating a world where every woman feels safe, empowered, and valued.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive support through education, empowerment, and direct assistance to survivors of gender-based violence.
